ABOUT US

We are one of the leading suppliers of wholesale all type of heavy power supply products, meeting the needs of end customers & contractors. Over the years we have built a vast clientele list, which includes MEP contractors, commercial and residential electrical contractors, as well as homeowners seeking quality products.
